About us

The organization hosts a range of inter-college and intra-college events, including Innerve (a 24-hour national-level Hackathon) and Anubhav (a platform for sharing experiences). Additionally, it offers classes on subjects like Web Development, Git & GitHub, and Machine Learning for students. The club actively engages in college projects and provides support to students undertaking individual projects. Its primary goal is to cultivate a vibrant coding and development culture while actively contributing to open source initiatives
